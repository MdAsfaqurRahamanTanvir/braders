INFORMATION ABOUT BREEDER

Breed: Lakeland Terrier
Breeder Prefix: KINETA
Experience: 12 years

Kineta Lakelands are bred to be outstanding examples of the breed. Established in 1990 in Canada, my successful breeding program came "down under" in 2002. Recognised in showrings in Canada, America, and now Australia - Kineta Lakelands have been awarded by judges from all over the world. My small, selective breeding program boasts multiple Canadian, American, and Australian Champions and Grand Champions that have won numerous Best in Show, Best Exhibit in Group, and Best in Specialty Show awards. Since 2002, Kineta Lakeland Terriers have been ranked among the best Terriers in Australia. However, it's their winning attitude that makes them ideal family companions. Outgoing, confident, and cheeky, Lakelands possess an irresistable charisma. My Lakelands are available to approved homes on a non-breeding agreement. They come with a health guarantee and a money back satisfaction guarantee. Enquiries welcome!
